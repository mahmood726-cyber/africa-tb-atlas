We audited whether modern MDR/XDR-TB trials using bedaquiline, pretomanid, or linezolid since 2012-12-28 reach Cochrane syntheses and whether African-recruiting sites are equally represented.
Of 50 eligible trials in ClinicalTrials.gov or WHO ICTRP since bedaquiline FDA-EUA (2012-12-28), 35 included at least one African site versus 15 without.
Each trial passed a four-gate funnel (registration, results-posting, peer-publication, Cochrane inclusion via Pairwise70 NCT/ISRCTN-bridge), pre-registered via signed GitHub tag and Internet Archive snapshot.
Cochrane inclusion was 25.7% for Africa-recruiting versus 0.0% for non-Africa; patient-weighted the gap was 35.6% versus 0.0%, leaving 7,202 African-recruited patient-trial-arms outside any synthesis.
